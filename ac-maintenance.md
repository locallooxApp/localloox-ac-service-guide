# AC Maintenance Guide

Regular maintenance helps avoid sudden breakdowns and keeps cooling efficient.

## ✅ Basic Checklist

- Clean filters every 2–3 weeks  
- Check airflow and vents  
- Inspect outdoor unit  
- Monitor cooling performance  

## 💡 Why It Matters

- Better cooling efficiency  
- Lower electricity usage  
- Longer AC life
